Life Insurance

Life Insurance

"Protect your family's future"

Life insurance is a unique financial instrument that assures the creation of a desired amount of liquid capital at death. It provides essential protection for your loved ones.

Primary Uses:
  • Income for surviving family members
  • Federal and state death tax payment
  • Debt repayment protection
  • Children's education funding
  • Wealth transfer between generations

Education

Educational Savings

"Invest in your child's future"

Coverdell Education Savings Accounts help you save for education expenses with tax advantages. Contributions allowed until child turns 18 with a $2,000 annual limit.

Key Features:
  • Tax-free withdrawals for education
  • Transferable to other children
  • Guaranteed cash values
  • K-12 and college expenses
  • Open with just $100
