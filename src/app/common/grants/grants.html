Education Grant Program

Expanding Opportunities

The Education Grant Program expands educational opportunities for PFSA members pursuing higher education without discrimination.

This program provides financial assistance to members who may not qualify for traditional scholarships but are committed to furthering their education at accredited institutions.

No Minimum GPA Required
Year-Round Applications
No Financial Need Proof
Vocational Schools Included

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a PFSA benefit member in good standing
  • Current dues paid for two years prior to application
  • Accepted to an accredited university, college, or vocational/trade school
  • Must not have received a previous PFSA Grant or have an open PFSA Scholarship
