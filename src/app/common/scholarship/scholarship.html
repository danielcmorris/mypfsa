PFSA Scholarship Foundation

Empowering Future Leaders

The Portuguese Fraternal Society of America Scholarship Foundation was established in 2010 as a nonprofit organization operating under IRC Section 501(c)(3).

The foundation serves charitable purposes with a commitment to encourage qualified students financially, without discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex, or religious preference.

Foster Education

Providing scholarship grants to qualified students pursuing their academic dreams.

Promote Higher Education

Supporting students continuing their education beyond high school.

Memorial & Donor Funds

Accepting special memorial and in-honor funds disbursed according to donor wishes.
