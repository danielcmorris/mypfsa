Apply for a Scholarship

The PFSA Scholarship Foundation offers funding in three categories to help students achieve their educational goals.

Four & Two-Year Scholarships

For PFSA members meeting eligibility requirements pursuing higher education.

Joseph Fagundes Memorial

Open to non-members who are Humboldt County, California residents.

Ernie Mendes Scholarship

For applicants pursuing agriculture or science studies.

Eligibility Requirements

  • PFSA Members for two years prior to application deadline
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA for four-year institutions
  • Minimum 2.5 GPA for two-year/vocational schools
  • High school graduates entering accredited institutions as full-time students
Application Deadline: March 1
