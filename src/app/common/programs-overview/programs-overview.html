PFSA Programs

Committed to Community

The Portuguese Fraternal Society of America promotes cultural, social, educational, and charitable activities in local communities.

With 30,000 members organized into 65 Fraternal Councils, PFSA has been committed since its foundation to giving back to the community. Our members are encouraged to "Make a Difference" through various participation opportunities that support those in need.
