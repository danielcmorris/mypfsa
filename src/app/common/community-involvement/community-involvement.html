Community Involvement

Making an Impact

Since the merger of four Portuguese Fraternal Societies in January 2010, PFSA has expanded its community engagement through member volunteer hours and financial contributions.

PFSA works with communities to support events, fundraisers, and local festivals. Our members donate their time and resources to various organizations, raising monetary donations for scholarship funds and supporting those in need.

Portuguese Organizations

Supporting cultural institutions including the Portuguese Historical Museum, Five Wounds Portuguese National Parish, and heritage organizations across California.

Community & Charity

Contributing to Habitat for Humanity, food banks, Valley Children's Hospital, Kaiser, Meals On Wheels, and numerous community organizations.

Cultural Events

Portuguese Heritage Nights with SF Giants, San Jose Sharks, and Oakland A's. Culture symposiums and official visits by Portugal's leadership.

Youth Programs

Supporting youth sports teams and groups including soccer clubs, baseball teams, and school programs throughout California.
