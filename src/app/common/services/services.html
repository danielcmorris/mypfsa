Financial Planning Options with PFSA

Insurance & Investment

Whole Life

Provides permanent lifetime coverage with guaranteed premiums, guaranteed death benefits, and a built-in cash-value account that grows over time, creating a tax-advantaged financial asset you can use during your lifetime

Read More

Term Life

Provides affordable coverage for a specific period of time, such as 10, 20, or 30 years. Designed to protect your family during their highest-need years, like while raising children or paying off a mortgage.

Read More

Universal Life

Universal life insurance provides permanent coverage with flexible premiums and a cash-value account that grows based on credited interest. It allows you to adjust payments and coverage over time while maintaining lifelong financial protection.

Read More

Annuities

Annuities convert your savings into a reliable stream of income you cannot outlive, helping protect against longevity and market risk. They provide tax-deferred growth and can be structured to deliver guaranteed payments for life or for a fixed period.

Read More

IRAs

These tax-advantaged retirement accounts let you save and invest for the future while reducing current or future taxes. They help you build long-term wealth through compounding and disciplined, protected investing.

Read More

Education Savings Account

A Coverdell Education Savings Account is a tax-advantaged way to save for a child’s education, allowing investments to grow tax-free when used for qualified expenses. It can be used for both K–12 and college costs, giving families flexible, long-term education funding.
